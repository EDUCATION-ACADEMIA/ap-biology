Which of the following is a phenotype?

(A) Aa

(B) ** red hair

(C) heterozygous

(D) BB

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Red hair is the null phenotype of MC1R.

Concept note-2: -RHC phenotype is characterized by fair pigmentation (fair skin, red hair and freckles), and by sun sensitivity (poor tanning response and solar lentigines).

Concept note-3: -A phenotype is your version of a trait. Like curly vs straight hair. An allele is the term geneticists use to refer to a version of a gene. For example, a hair texture gene might come in two versions: curly or straight.

Concept note-4: -This gene has several versions (alleles), one of which is related to red hair. Red hair is a recessive trait, which means that only those who get two “redhead” versions of the gene, one from the mother and one from the father, will have red hair.

Concept note-5: -In humans, phenotype examples include earwax type, height, blood type, eye color, freckles, and hair color.
