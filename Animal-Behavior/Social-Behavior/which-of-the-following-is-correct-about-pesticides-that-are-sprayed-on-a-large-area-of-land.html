Which of the following is correct about pesticides that are sprayed on a large area of land?

(A) It will accumulate in primary producers

(B) It will accumulate in primary consumers

(C) ** It will accumulate in most in top predators that feed on all of the lower trophic levels

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The label is the most important source of information for safe pesticide use. Read it carefully and familiarise yourself with all instructions and warnings before you use the pesticide. The label tells you all you need to know about the product, including usage, mixing and safety instructions.

Concept note-2: -Dermal (absorption through skin or eyes) In most work situations, absorption through the skin is the most common route of pesticide exposure.

Concept note-3: -Among the different pesticides used in the agricultural fields, glyphosate is the most used active pesticides since 2001, followed by atrazine and metolachlor-S.

Concept note-4: -The organism present at the highest trophic level or the top of the food chain accumulates the maximum amount of the pesticide because this is a non-degradable toxic chemical.
